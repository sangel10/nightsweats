Night Sweats is comedy production company located in Berlin. We are dedicated to fostering talent here Berlin and bringing the top comedians from around the world here to perform.

We run a weekly open mic, if you'd like to perform please sign-up on the Facebook event. If you're an experienced comic and are interested in headlining please contact us.

We also book shows featuring international acts. Subscribe to our newsletter to hear about upcoming shows.
